About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Working with academic partners and industry practitioners, IDR bridges the gap between academic insight and real-world implementation through training, advisory and innovation.

IDR Model

Building digital risk capability through training, innovation and industry collaboration.

Academy

Training programmes and bootcamps designed for students and professionals to develop expertise in cyber, AI and digital risk management.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new digital risk models, governance frameworks and research for emerging technologies including AI systems and digital infrastructure.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement best-practice frameworks such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 for digital resilience and cybersecurity.
